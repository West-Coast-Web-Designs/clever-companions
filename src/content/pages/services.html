Fear & Reactivity Training

Our Fear and Reactivity Training program is tailored to address the specific needs of dogs struggling with fearful or reactive behaviors. Whether your dog exhibits anxiety, barking, lunging, or other reactive responses towards other dogs, people, or certain triggers, our compassionate approach aims to help them overcome these challenges. Through personalized sessions and positive reinforcement techniques, we work closely with you and your furry friend to build confidence, promote calm behavior, and ultimately foster a happier and more relaxed companion. With our guidance and support, you'll gain valuable insights into your dog's behavior and learn effective strategies to navigate challenging situations, strengthening the bond between you and your beloved pet.

Our Fear and Reactivity Training isn't just about addressing immediate behavior issues—it's about empowering both you and your dog for long-term success. By providing practical tools and emotional support, we help you navigate the journey towards a more harmonious relationship with your furry companion. Together, we'll embark on a transformative journey that not only improves your dog's behavior but also deepens the bond of trust and understanding between you, ensuring a lifetime of companionship filled with joy and confidence.

Basic Obedience

At Clever Companions, our Basic Obedience Training program lays the foundation for a well-behaved and harmonious relationship between you and your furry friend. From mastering essential skills like sit, stay, and come, to refining leash manners and polite greetings, our personalized one-on-one sessions are designed to cater to your dog's individual needs. With a focus on positive reinforcement and effective communication techniques, we empower you to strengthen the bond with your canine companion while instilling confidence and obedience.

Our Basic Obedience Training isn't just about teaching commands—it's about building a solid foundation of trust and understanding. Through patience, consistency, and encouragement, we guide you and your dog through the training process, creating a positive and rewarding experience for both of you. Whether you're starting with a new puppy or seeking to improve your adult dog's manners, our program equips you with the tools and knowledge to nurture a well-mannered and obedient companion for life.

Adventure Walks

We believe that every dog deserves an enriching and fulfilling experience, which is why we offer Adventure Walks that go beyond the traditional stroll around the block. Our guided excursions are tailored to provide your furry friend with opportunities for mental stimulation, physical exercise, and exploration in new and exciting environments. Whether it's a hike in the woods, a leisurely walk around town, or a romp through a local park, our Adventure Walks are designed to cater to your dog's individual needs and preferences.

During our Adventure Walks, your dog will not only enjoy the sights, sounds, and smells of their surroundings but also benefit from positive reinforcement training techniques incorporated into the experience. Led by our experienced trainers, these outings provide opportunities for your dog to practice good behavior in real-world settings while strengthening their bond with you. With Clever Companions, you can rest assured that your furry friend will embark on an adventure filled with joy, enrichment, and unforgettable experiences.
